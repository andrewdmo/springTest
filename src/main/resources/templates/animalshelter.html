Animal Shelter

Animal shelter was our first full Java build. The fauna-saving title is midleading as an Atari game's: promising but overstates simple gameplay, and the fun doesn't last.


What does persist are indexed Animal objects! Starting with a simple ArrayList, across 6 different versions storage includes local JSON and pSQL storage. A.S. also bloomed from being text-based into JSP and full web versions.


I fell behind building a working version but learned a ton. Sadly the Shelter is closed but should re-open soon.
