Nori Retail App

Nori was our final project in Backend Engineering. She's not far from being discharged by the surgery triage, but currently still pics must suffice. She boasted the only real-time functionality of all the projects.


A one-way POST request is triggered instantly by a sale completion at a Shopify store. This Webhook prompts the app (packaged JAR w/ Tomcat) to update its local pSQL tables and send an event-specific email.


The idea was that by setting specific levels for (high or low selling) items, a store owner could master their own on-demand inventory supply. Live demo soon
